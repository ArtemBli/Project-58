My Favorite Hobby

Playing Soccer


About


My favorite hobby is to play soccer. I've been playing soccer since I was 5 years old. When I first started playing soccer I didn't really like to play because I wasn't good at it and I always kept losing during games. But as I went to more practices I started getting better and better and actually enjoying playing now. I also like soccer because if I have nothing to do and I'm bored in the house then I can go outside and practice soccer by myself.
